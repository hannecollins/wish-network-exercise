🌟 Wish Network Exercise 🌟

Exploring Social Networks and Weak Ties

📋 Instructions for Professor

  1. Sections: Edit the section list (one per line), then save. Use a short id and optional label, e.g. 3|Tuesday or just 4 for default label "Section 4".
  2. Student names: Choose a section, then enter all student names for that section (one per line) and save.
  3. Phase 1 - Make a Wish: Have students fill out:
    • Who they talk to most in the section
    • Their wish (what they want to learn/do)
  4. Phase 2 - Grant Wishes: After all wishes are collected, have students:
    • View all wishes from their classmates
    • Select 1-3 wishes they can grant
  5. View Networks: Generate and display the two network diagrams
Each line: id or id|label. Ids are stored in data and in students.json keys. Labels appear in dropdowns for students.
